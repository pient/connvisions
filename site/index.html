Connecting people is our business
Connecting Visions was eatablished and with our head office in HongKong. In 2013 we set up a branch office in ShenZhen, China. Our business is connecting people from all over the world and within China. We specialize in Education, Music and Events; helping people reach their full potential and educating others to do the same.
Our company has a well established reputation and we work with many different companies and schools to provide the best services in education and entertainment. Our work fully supports international communication and we aim to reach as many people as possible across the globe.
Connecting Visions offers a fantastic programs for new teachers, musicians and artists. Our goal is to bring people together and support them on journeys, and help them progress as individuals.
We pride ourseleves on being reputable company with many connections across China and the world, known for our loyalty and ability to provide services to cater to many different people.
